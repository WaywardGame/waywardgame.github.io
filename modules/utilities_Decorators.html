Debounce

  • Debounce(time: number, initial?: boolean, returnIfCancelled?: (...args: any[]) => any): (target: any, key: any, descriptor: any) => { configurable: boolean; get: any; set: any }
    • Defined in src/game/utilities/Decorators.ts:42

    Prevents the decorated method from being called at a high speed. Example:

    1. Method is called.
    2. ms amount of time is waited. If the method is called again in this time, cancel.
    3. If the method was not called after ms, actually call the method.

    WARNING: This also binds the method to the host, a la @Bound. Do not use both decorators. WARNING 2: Only the arguments of the last call are passed along!!
